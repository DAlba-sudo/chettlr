Why is this bad?

This is bad because in this position, white is forked. Can you guess the evaluation? A stunning -0.6. Why is that?

  1. White must lose a tempo saving the bishop, but doing so undevelops the bishop and loses the knight (while Black is now temporarily up a pawn!).
  2. Black is temporarily up a center pawn.
